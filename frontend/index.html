About Infinite Helix

Empowering individuals with AI-driven health insights

Our Mission

Infinite Helix aims to democratize healthcare by making medical report analysis accessible to everyone. We use advanced AI technologies to help you understand your health better.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python FastAPI
  • OCR: Tesseract OCR
  • NLP: spaCy with medical models
  • Database: PostgreSQL (Neon)
  • Frontend: Modern HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

üöÄ Advanced ML Features (Backend)

Our system includes enterprise-grade AI capabilities:

  • BioBERT Entity Extraction: Fine-tuned medical entity recognition
  • Medication NER: Custom named entity recognition for drugs
  • Anomaly Detection: Ensemble ML model (95.9% accuracy) trained on 100K+ medical samples
  • Longitudinal Analysis: Health trend tracking over time
  • GPU Training: NVIDIA CUDA-accelerated model training
  • Real Datasets: 8 verified medical datasets from Hugging Face
